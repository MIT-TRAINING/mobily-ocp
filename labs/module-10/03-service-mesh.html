Concept · Service Mesh

A proxy beside every pod — security & routing, no code changes.

A Service Mesh injects an Envoy sidecar into each pod (the data plane), programmed by Istiod (the control plane). That gives you mTLS and traffic routing — like canary releases — without touching app code.

Sidecar injection & mTLS

Toggle injection and watch pods gain an Envoy sidecar (READY goes 1/1 → 2/2), then require mTLS.

The app is unaware — all its traffic transparently flows through its Envoy sidecar (the data plane), which Istiod (the control plane) configures. mTLS, retries, and telemetry happen in the proxy.
