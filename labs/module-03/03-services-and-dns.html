Concept · Services, endpoints & DNS

Pods come and go. The Service name stays.

A Service is a stable name + virtual IP over a changing set of pods. It finds them by label selector and stores the result as an EndpointSlice — its live backend list. CoreDNS makes it reachable as <svc>.<ns>.svc.cluster.local.

Service: subscriber-api  selector: app=subscriber-api

Click a pod to toggle its readiness; click the label to toggle whether it matches the selector. Watch the EndpointSlice react.

EndpointSlice (live backends, port 8080)

Only pods that match the selector AND are Ready become endpoints. An empty slice = the Service has nothing to route to (connection refused) — the #1 "my Service is broken" cause.
