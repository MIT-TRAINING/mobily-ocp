Concept · Rolling updates & rollbacks

Ship a new version without dropping a request.

A Deployment manages one ReplicaSet per version, so it can shift replicas old→new gradually — bounded by maxSurge and maxUnavailable so capacity never dips below your guarantee. And the old ReplicaSet stays parked at zero, which is what makes rollback instant.

What you're seeing

New (1.26) pods come up Ready before old (1.25) pods leave. With maxUnavailable=0 the Deployment must surge first; with maxSurge=0 it must drain first. Ready count stays at/above the guaranteed minimum throughout.
The old ReplicaSet isn't deleted — it's scaled to 0 and kept. That parked recipe is why rollback is one command.
