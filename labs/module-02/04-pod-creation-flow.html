The one takeaway

No actor skipped a step or talked out of band. Every arrow went through the API server; etcd recorded each transition; each actor reacted to a watch, not a direct call. That decoupling is what makes Kubernetes scalable, resilient, and extensible — you can add new controllers without touching the others.
