Why order & dependencies matter

Secret & PVC first — the DB won't start without its credentials or durable storage. Services give pods stable addresses; the Route is useless without a Service behind it (empty endpoints → 503). The NetworkPolicy guards who may reach the api. Monitoring watches it; Argo CD makes the whole thing declarative & recoverable from Git.
This is the entire course in one object graph: Route (M6) → Service (M3) → Deployment (M3) → Secret (M7) → PVC/PV (M7), in a Project (M8), guarded by NetworkPolicy (M6), watched by Prometheus (M11), delivered by GitOps (M10), recoverable via etcd backup + troubleshooting (M12).
