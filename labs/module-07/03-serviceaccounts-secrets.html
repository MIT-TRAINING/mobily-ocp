Concept · Identity & secrets

A pod needs an identity — and a safe way to get its password.

Every pod runs as a ServiceAccount; RBAC and SCC attach to it. And credentials belong in a Secret, not the image — but remember, a Secret is base64, not encryption. Explore both.

Secret: base64 is NOT encryption

Create a Secret, then reveal how trivially its "encoded" value decodes. Anyone who can oc get it can read it.

// create a secret to see its stored form
A Secret stores values base64-encoded — reversible by anyone, not encrypted. Protection comes from RBAC (who can read it) and optional etcd encryption at rest. Never commit real secret values to Git.
