What you'll be able to explain afterwards

  • The four networks (node / pod / service / external) and how a request flows user → router → Service → pod
  • How Services + CoreDNS give pods stable identity, and why ClusterIP is the OpenShift norm
  • How Routes expose apps, and where TLS terminates for edge, passthrough and re-encrypt — plus Route vs Ingress
  • How NetworkPolicies work (additive, allow-only, default-deny) and why a policy-less pod is wide open
  • How to combine Project + RBAC + quota + NetworkPolicy into real multi-tenant isolation
